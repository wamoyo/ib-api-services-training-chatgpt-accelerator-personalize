Style Suggestions for AI Generated Images

Use these style prompts with AI image generators like DALL-E, Midjourney, or Stable Diffusion to create images that match Best Thrive's brand.

Warm Educational Photography

For authentic, welcoming imagery that feels real and relatable

Use warm, natural photography with soft lighting and authentic expressions. Keep the color palette bright and cheerful with golden yellow accents (#ffc500). Make it feel optimistic and uplifting with professional photographic quality. Use natural settings with diverse representation.

Playful Illustration Style

For fun, engaging graphics that appeal to children

Use a playful, colorful illustration style with rounded shapes and friendly characters. Incorporate golden yellow (#ffc500) and warm tones. Make it vibrant and energetic with clean vector art. Keep it whimsical but professional with inclusive character design.

Clean Educational Graphics

For infographics and educational materials

Use a modern, minimalist design with clean lines and simple shapes. Incorporate brand colors: golden yellow (#cc9e00), medium gray (#737373), dark gray (#4d4d4d). Make it professional and clear with flat design principles. Use geometric icons and ample whitespace.

Warm Gradient Style

For backgrounds and accent elements

Use soft gradient backgrounds with warm golden yellow (#ffc500) transitioning to cream (#fff9e6). Keep it subtle and professional with smooth color transitions. Add subtle textures for depth without overpowering the design. Make it calming and uplifting.
