Who is
Nakia Whittaker?

She is a Mother Daughter Friend Advocate Admin Pro Geek Allthethings

Its ME! Im Nakia D. Whittaker, and my story is rooted in extended family, opportunities, growth, and resilience.

In 2018, I was ready to redefine and navigate from Income to Impact on my termsaligned with my values and abilities. Thats when the idea of using the passion for my career to become a Virtual Assistant sparked my curiosity.

But lets be realbreaking into entrepreneurship as a woman, especially as a woman of color, the resources were chaotic, guarded, and expensive. Where was the How-To guide for us? Where was the community? Where was the authentic and real advice for us? Sound familiar? I remember Googling how to start a business and being greeted by 5 BILLION search results.

more about me later…..
