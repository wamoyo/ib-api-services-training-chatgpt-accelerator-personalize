Sentence Structure

Word Choice

Your vocabulary is accessible yet aspirational. You use simple, powerful verbs ("redefine," "navigate," "empower," "transform") and avoid corporate jargon. Your language is action-oriented and transformation-focused.

Perspective

You write in first person dominant when sharing your story ("I'm Nakia," "I was ready to redefine") and shift to "we/our" when discussing collective mission, creating both relatable founder connection and inclusive movement feel.
