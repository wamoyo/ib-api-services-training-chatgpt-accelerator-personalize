Your Top Competitors

Based on market analysis, here are the platforms and organizations competing for your customers in the women's entrepreneurship space:

SCORE

score.org/women-entrepreneurs

What They Do: Non-profit providing free expert resources, tools, and mentorship designed to support women at every stage of their entrepreneurial journey.

Target Market: Women entrepreneurs seeking practical guidance, funding insights, and one-on-one counseling at no cost.

Key Strength: Established national presence with volunteer mentors and completely free services backed by SBA.

Market Position: Free but generalist approach vs. your specialized focus on authentic empowerment for female founders.

BossBabe/Société

bossbabe.com

What They Do: Private community for women entrepreneurs aiming to scale their businesses with tools, training, and a supportive network to achieve six-figure incomes.

Target Market: Ambitious women entrepreneurs focused on rapid revenue growth and scaling to 6-7 figures.

Key Strength: Large, engaged community with proven framework for revenue growth and business scaling.

Market Position: High-profile brand focused on revenue goals vs. your holistic approach to legacy building and transformative relationships.

Ellevate Network

ellevatenetwork.com

What They Do: Global community for women+ who know there is strength in numbers, offering networking, mentorship, and career advancement resources.

Target Market: Professional women and entrepreneurs seeking networking opportunities and peer support globally.

Key Strength: Established global network with corporate partnerships and certified as Women's Business Enterprise.

Market Position: Broader professional network vs. your specific focus on female founders and entrepreneurship ecosystem.

Women 2.0

women2.com

What They Do: Community focused on women in technology and entrepreneurship, offering events, mentorship, and pitch competitions to help female founders secure funding.

Target Market: Women tech founders seeking venture funding and connections to investors.

Key Strength: Strong focus on fundraising and investor connections, particularly in tech sector.

Market Position: Tech-focused fundraising platform vs. your multi-industry ecosystem with authentic business guidance.

She Runs It

sherunsit.org

What They Do: Non-profit serving women in marketing, media, and tech industries with 6,000+ members globally, offering networking events, mentoring programs, and education grants.

Target Market: Women in marketing, media, and technology sectors seeking industry-specific networking and mentorship.

Key Strength: Industry-specific focus with established reputation in marketing/media/tech verticals.

Market Position: Industry-specific non-profit vs. your cross-industry entrepreneurial ecosystem with virtual assistance and business services.

Virtual Assistant Platforms (Time etc, MyWorksy)

timeetc.com / myworksy.com

What They Do: On-demand virtual assistant matching services connecting businesses with pre-vetted VAs for administrative and business support tasks.

Target Market: Business owners and entrepreneurs needing delegated administrative support without hiring full-time staff.

Key Strength: Fast matching, established VA pools, streamlined onboarding, and transparent pricing models.

Market Position: Transactional VA service providers vs. your holistic ecosystem combining VA services with mentorship, community, and legacy building.
