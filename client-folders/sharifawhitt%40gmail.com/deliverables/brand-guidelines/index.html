Brand Overview

"Empowering children to thrive and succeed"

Best Thrive is an educational services organization based in Rochester, New York, dedicated to helping children reach their full potential through personalized learning experiences.

We believe in the power of fun to transform lives, providing culturally representative educational products that help children build high self-esteem while learning with characters that look like them.

Industry: Educational Services, Child Development, Tutoring

Target Market: Children from diverse backgrounds and their families seeking academic and professional development support

Brand Voice & Messaging

Key Messaging

"Empowering children to thrive and succeed"

Core Value Propositions

  • The power of fun: We transform lives through joyful, engaging learning experiences
  • Personalized approach: Tailored programs that help each child reach their full potential
  • Culturally representative: Learning with characters and content that reflect diverse backgrounds
  • Building self-esteem: Empowering children with confidence and high self-worth
  • Experienced educators: Professional team committed to academic and professional success

Target Audience

Parents and families seeking educational support for children from diverse backgrounds, who value personalized learning that celebrates cultural identity while building academic excellence and self-confidence.

Writing Style

Best Thrive's tone is warm, encouraging, and professional. We balance playfulness with credibility, making families feel welcome while demonstrating educational expertise.

DO

  • Use warm, friendly language
  • Emphasize fun and transformation
  • Celebrate diversity and inclusion
  • Focus on empowerment and potential
  • Keep sentences clear and accessible
  • Use "we" and "you" for connection

DON'T

  • Use overly academic jargon
  • Sound cold or impersonal
  • Make assumptions about backgrounds
  • Focus on limitations or deficits
  • Use complex, bureaucratic language
  • Adopt a distant, corporate tone

Brand Usage Guidelines

DO

  • Use approved brand colors consistently
  • Maintain clear space around logo
  • Use Montserrat and Lato fonts
  • Keep messaging focused on empowerment
  • Showcase diversity in imagery
  • Use bright yellow for CTAs
  • Maintain warm, friendly tone
  • Emphasize fun and transformation

DON'T

  • Alter brand colors or create new ones
  • Distort or resize logo incorrectly
  • Use fonts outside the brand system
  • Use deficit-based language
  • Use non-diverse stock imagery
  • Use dull colors for important CTAs
  • Adopt corporate or cold tone
  • Downplay the role of fun in learning
